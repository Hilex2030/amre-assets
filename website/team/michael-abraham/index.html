About Michael

A different lens on Los Angeles real estate.

Michael Abraham brings a rare combination to Los Angeles real estate: the eye of a licensed architect and the instincts of a seasoned investor. As Principal Agent of AMRE Real Estate Group at Compass Beverly Hills, Michael advises sellers, buyers, and investors across the city's most sought-after neighborhoods — and is known for integrity, strategic thinking, and a client-first approach that runs deeper than the transaction.

His architectural training shapes everything from how he reads a floor plan to how he evaluates the long-term value of a Los Angeles home. Where most agents see square footage, Michael sees structural integrity, light, flow, renovation potential, and the design decisions that quietly determine resale value years from now. For buyers, that means honest counsel on what's worth pursuing — and what isn't. For sellers, it means a marketing strategy grounded in the property's actual architectural merits.

Beyond single-family advisory, Michael has spent over a decade investing in Los Angeles multifamily real estate, specializing in value-add and 1031-exchange strategies that unlock hidden upside. He counsels investor clients on rent control compliance, deferred-maintenance underwriting, and the long-game arithmetic of cash-on-cash versus appreciation — the kind of analysis that turns a marginal deal into a generational one.

Michael partners with Ania De Pourbaix on every transaction. Clients get two senior agents on every deal — never a junior assistant. The pair operates with one shared belief: the most important real estate decisions deserve advisors who think like clients, not just transact like agents.
