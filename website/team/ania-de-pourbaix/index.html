About Ania

A different lens on Los Angeles real estate.

Ania De Pourbaix is a seasoned Los Angeles Realtor® whose calm command of the transaction has earned her a deeply loyal client base across the city's most competitive markets. As Principal Agent at AMRE Real Estate Group at Compass Beverly Hills, Ania anchors every deal with meticulous attention to detail, strategic negotiation, and the kind of concierge-level service most clients have never experienced before.

Her expertise spans the LA Westside luxury corridor — Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Brentwood, Santa Monica, and Malibu — where she's guided buyers and sellers through some of the most consequential transactions of their lives. Whether the property is a first home or a generational estate, Ania brings the same standard: she reads the room, she reads the contract, and she advocates relentlessly until the door is closed.

Following the Palisades Fire, Ania became a trusted advisor to displaced families navigating the most difficult chapter of their housing history. She combined early off-market lead generation with patient, emotionally intelligent guidance — finding homes for clients who had lost everything, and doing it with grace.

Ania partners with Michael Abraham on every transaction. Clients get two principal-level agents on every deal — Ania's intuition and negotiation expertise paired with Michael's architectural eye and investment fluency. The two operate as one team with one shared standard: clarity, integrity, and unrelenting advocacy on behalf of the families and investors who trust them.
