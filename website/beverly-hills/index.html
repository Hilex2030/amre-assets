About Beverly Hills

A market with character.

Beverly Hills is not one market — it's three. The flats, with their tree-lined streets and tightly-zoned lots, attract a different buyer than the foothills above Sunset, where view, privacy, and architectural pedigree command material premiums. Trousdale Estates, with its mid-century modernism and dramatic sight lines, is its own conversation entirely. Working in Beverly Hills demands knowing which conversation you're in.

AMRE Real Estate Group represents buyers and sellers across all three sub-markets, with an architecture-trained perspective that's particularly relevant in a market defined by period revival mansions, mid-century icons, and contemporary trophy properties. Whether you're acquiring on the flats, selling a Trousdale residence, or evaluating a Coldwater Canyon view property, we bring the technical eye and negotiating discipline these transactions require.

Trousdale & Foothills

The Flats

90210 vs. 90212
