Common Questions

What areas of Los Angeles does AMRE serve?

AMRE serves all of Los Angeles County with expertise in Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Brentwood, Santa Monica, Venice, West Hollywood, Studio City, Malibu, and the Hollywood Hills.

What is the Compass 3-Phase Marketing Strategy?

Phase 1 — Private Exclusive: your home marketed to 34,000+ Compass agents, no public MLS days. Phase 2 — Coming Soon: visible on Compass.com. Phase 3 — Full MLS Launch with market-tested pricing. Achieves 2.9% higher prices and closes 20% faster on average.

AMRE combines 25 years of expertise across real estate, architecture, mortgage lending, interior design, and digital marketing. Michael's architectural background and Ania's lending expertise deliver a 360-degree approach no single-discipline agent can match.
