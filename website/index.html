FAQ

Common questions.

What areas of Los Angeles does AMRE Real Estate Group serve?

AMRE serves all of Los Angeles County with particular expertise in Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Brentwood, Santa Monica, Venice, West Hollywood, Studio City, Malibu, and the Hollywood Hills. We specialize in luxury residential and multifamily investment properties throughout the LA Basin.

What is the Compass 3-Phase Marketing Strategy?

The Compass 3-Phase Strategy involves: Phase 1 — Private Exclusive (marketing to 34,000+ Compass agents before public listing), Phase 2 — Coming Soon (public visibility on Compass.com without MLS days-on-market), and Phase 3 — Full MLS Launch (Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com with market-tested pricing). This approach typically achieves 2.9% higher sale prices and 20% faster closings compared to direct-to-MLS listings.

How does Compass Concierge work?

Compass Concierge fronts the cost of pre-sale home improvements — staging, painting, landscaping, kitchen and bathroom updates, and more — with zero interest and zero fees. You repay only when your home sells. This allows sellers to maximize their sale price without any upfront out-of-pocket costs.

Does AMRE work with buyers as well as sellers?

Yes. We serve both buyers and sellers with equal commitment. For buyers, we provide access to the full market including off-market and Compass Private Exclusive listings, expert negotiation, and our unique architectural perspective on property evaluation. Michael Abraham's background as a licensed architect gives buyers a distinct advantage when assessing structural integrity, renovation potential, and true market value.
