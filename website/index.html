Common Questions

Frequently asked.

What areas of Los Angeles does AMRE serve?

AMRE serves all of Los Angeles County with particular expertise in Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Brentwood, Santa Monica, Venice, West Hollywood, Studio City, Malibu, and the Hollywood Hills. We specialize in luxury residential and multifamily investment properties throughout the LA Basin.

What is the Compass 3-Phase Marketing Strategy?

Phase 1 — Private Exclusive: your home is marketed to 34,000+ Compass agents with no public MLS days accumulating. Phase 2 — Coming Soon: visible on Compass.com without MLS days-on-market. Phase 3 — Full MLS Launch with market-tested pricing. This approach typically achieves 2.9% higher sale prices and closes 20% faster.

What makes AMRE different from other Los Angeles agents?

AMRE combines over 30 years of experience across real estate, architecture, mortgage lending, interior design, and digital marketing. Michael's architectural background provides a unique advantage evaluating property potential and renovation value. Ania's lending expertise ensures clients understand the full financial picture. Together they offer a 360-degree approach no single-discipline agent can match.

Does AMRE work with real estate investors?

Yes. Investment properties — particularly Los Angeles multifamily — are a core specialty. Michael has personally invested in LA multifamily properties for over a decade and brings deep expertise in value-add strategies, 1031 exchanges, cap rate analysis, and off-market sourcing to every investor client.
