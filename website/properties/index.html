Searching LA

Common questions, answered.

Are these all the homes available in Los Angeles?

No — the properties displayed represent AMRE's active and recently closed work, plus a curated selection of Compass listings. A material portion of LA inventory trades through off-market and Compass Private Exclusive channels that never reach a public website. Reach out for a personalized search tailored to your budget, neighborhood, and timeline.

How can I see Compass Private Exclusives that aren't listed publicly?

Compass Private Exclusives are off-market listings shared only inside the Compass agent network before they hit the MLS. As a Compass-represented buyer, you gain visibility into properties that most buyers never see. Schedule a buyer consultation and we'll share relevant Private Exclusives matching your criteria.

Can you help me find a property that's not on this site?

Yes. Most of our buy-side work begins with a custom search across the full MLS, Compass Private Exclusives, pocket listings, and direct off-market outreach. Whether you're looking in Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Santa Monica, Culver City, Mid-City, West Adams, or anywhere across LA County, we'll surface inventory that fits your specifications.

How often is the inventory on this page updated?

Active listings sync regularly from Compass and the MLS. For the most current availability and pricing on a specific property, contact the team directly — public websites lag the MLS by hours to days, and competitive properties often move before public data refreshes.

Do you only represent buyers, or sellers too?

Both. AMRE represents sellers (with the Compass three-phase marketing strategy and Compass Concierge home-prep financing) and buyers (with off-market access and architectural-eye walkthroughs). For multifamily and 1031-exchange clients, we also run cash-flow underwriting and 1031 timelines.
