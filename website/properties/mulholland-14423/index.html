The Brief

Out of state. One option. Our task: handle everything.

Our client lived hundreds of miles away and didn't have the bandwidth — or the desire — to fly in for renovations, staging, showings, and signings. They wanted a clean handoff with someone they could trust to drive the entire sale to a strong outcome. We took the keys, built the plan, and ran it end to end.

Pre-Listing
01

Preparation & Renovations

We knew which updates would drive value — and we ran every one of them.

From day one, we treated the property like our own. We oversaw light renovations, professional staging, landscape refresh, and a complete interior prep — all coordinated remotely, all without the seller writing a single check upfront.

Every dollar deployed through Compass Concierge — interest-free, with zero out-of-pocket cost — was targeted at the updates that move buyers and lift price. The result was a home that didn't just photograph well; it walked through well.

Fast Results & Seamless Closing

Multiple offers in days. Closed 1.5% over asking — and the seller never had to fly in.

Strategic pricing, clean presentation, and focused marketing did exactly what they were designed to do. Multiple strong offers landed within the first week. We were in escrow in under 8 days on market.

From there, we coordinated inspections, disclosures, signings, and the final walkthrough remotely. The seller signed everything from home — and on closing day, the wire hit at $1,721,000. Another satisfied client.
