A rare top-floor corner penthouse offering exceptional natural light and privacy.

Located in a modern courtyard building just south of Ventura Boulevard in Studio City, this refined 3-bedroom, 2.5-bath residence spans approximately 1,705 square feet and features an open layout designed for comfortable living and effortless entertaining.

Sunlit living and dining spaces with southern exposure are complemented by Canadian maple hardwood floors, recessed lighting, and a fireplace that anchors the main living area. The open kitchen flows seamlessly into the living space and is equipped with high-quality, recently updated appliances.

The spacious primary suite includes a walk-in closet and a well-appointed en-suite bath. Two additional bedrooms offer flexibility for guests, work-from-home, or investment use.

As a top-floor corner unit, this penthouse benefits from enhanced privacy, reduced noise, and elevated sightlines. The building offers a secure, community-oriented setting close to the best of Studio City's dining, shopping, and entertainment.
