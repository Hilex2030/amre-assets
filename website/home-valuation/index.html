Why this isn't a Zestimate

Algorithms don't walk the property.

Automated valuation tools work off public records — sale prices, square footage, bed and bath counts. They don't see the $80K kitchen reno, the new ADU permit pending, the school boundary that changed last year, or the buyer pool that's been chasing every triplex in your zip code. Our valuations are hand-built by people who know the streets, the buyers, and the deals that didn't show up on Redfin.
