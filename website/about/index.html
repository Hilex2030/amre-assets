Our Methodology

How we work.

01

Architecture-trained eye

Michael's architecture license isn't a credential we cite — it's how we evaluate every property. Light, scale, circulation, structural integrity, and long-term renovation potential are baked into every walkthrough.

02

Compass Concierge advantage

For sellers, we deploy Compass's three-phase launch — Private Exclusive, Coming Soon, MLS — and Concierge financing for pre-listing improvements with no payment until close. The result: faster sales at higher prices.

03

Concierge end-to-end

From first walkthrough to keys at closing, we manage inspections, contractors, lenders, escrow, and every party in the transaction. Clients should focus on the decision; we handle the orchestration.

04

Long-term advisors

Most of our business comes from past clients and referrals. We're not optimizing for the next transaction — we're optimizing for the next twenty years. That changes how every conversation goes.

05

Interior design background

Ania's interior design background brings a curator's eye to every property — from staging that elevates buyer perception to renovation guidance that maximizes resale value. We help sellers prepare homes that photograph beautifully and show flawlessly, and we help buyers visualize a home's full potential before making the offer.

06

Investor-focused

We underwrite Los Angeles real estate the way investors do. Cap rate, cash-on-cash, value-add upside, 1031 exchanges, and multifamily fundamentals are part of every conversation — whether you're buying your first duplex or repositioning a portfolio across Beverly Hills, the Westside, and beyond.

About AMRE

Frequently asked.

Who is AMRE Real Estate Group?

AMRE Real Estate Group is a Los Angeles real estate advisory partnership at Compass Beverly Hills, led by Michael Abraham (Realtor®, Licensed Architect — DRE #02242095) and Ania De Pourbaix (Realtor® — DRE #01891438). The team represents buyers, sellers, and investors across Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Brentwood, Santa Monica, the Hollywood Hills, Culver City, Mid-City, West Adams, and architecturally significant properties throughout Los Angeles County.

What's the partnership structure between Michael and Ania?

Michael and Ania operate as a true two-principal team, with both partners actively engaged on every transaction. Michael leads architectural and design analysis on the physical product, while Ania anchors negotiation, lender coordination, and client advocacy through closing. Clients get two senior agents on every deal — not a single agent and an assistant.

What kinds of clients does AMRE typically work with?

First-time owner-occupants, move-up and luxury buyers, sellers preparing properties for market, multifamily and 1031-exchange investors, estate and trust transactions, and out-of-state or international buyers relocating to Los Angeles. Every client receives the same level of advisory service regardless of price point.

Where is AMRE's office located?

AMRE Real Estate Group is based at the Compass Beverly Hills office at 9454 Wilshire Boulevard, Suite 100, Beverly Hills, CA 90212. Meetings can be hosted in-office, on-site at properties, at clients' homes, or remotely via video — whatever fits the client's schedule and geography.

Is AMRE part of Compass?

Yes. AMRE Real Estate Group operates under Compass (CA DRE #01991628), the largest residential real estate brokerage in the United States by sales volume. This affiliation gives clients access to Compass Private Exclusives, the Compass Concierge home-prep program, and a 34,000+ agent network across 70+ metropolitan markets nationwide.
