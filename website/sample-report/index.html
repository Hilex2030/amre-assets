0290-Day Neighborhood Forecast

Where this market is heading.

West Adams has been one of LA's most volatile neighborhoods over the last 24 months — premium-priced renovations are still moving quickly, but unrenovated stock is sitting longer. Here's the real picture for your specific block.

West Adams Median Price (May '26)
$1.097M −3.1%

Median down ~3% YoY, but median $/sqft has compressed harder (−14.9%). Translation: bigger homes are softening more than smaller, well-positioned homes.

Days on Market
35 ▲ from 28

Median DOM has crept up from 28 to 35 days YoY. Hot homes (under $1M, renovated, well-staged) still go pending in 14–21 days.

Active Inventory
104 ▲ from 74

Inventory has grown ~40% YoY. More competition on the way. The window for a confidently-priced spring/summer listing closes by July.

Sale-to-List on Hot Homes
+5% over list

Properties priced correctly and presented well are still selling above list. Mispriced or unstaged homes are sitting 60+ days and seeing price cuts.

Our Forecast

A correctly-priced, well-presented listing on this block should expect 18–25 days on market with multiple offers, and a final sale 2–5% over list. A mispriced listing will sit 60+ days, require a price reduction, and close 3–6% under list. The difference between those two outcomes is, on this property, approximately $80,000–$120,000.

Why $949,000 (not $899K, not $999K)
  • $899K leaves money on the table. Comps with comparable square footage and a true 2BA (rare on this block — most are 2/1) support a ~$760+/sqft baseline, which is $950K before condition adjustments.
  • $999K pushes past the search-filter ceiling. Most West Adams buyers in your price tier filter "under $1M" — listing at $949K keeps you in every search result while signaling premium positioning.
  • The 2-bath is your differentiator. Seven of the twelve comps above are 2/1. A true 2/2 in a 1923 Craftsman is structurally uncommon and warrants the $20–30K premium baked into the recommendation.
  • The block is appreciating around you. 2821 S Harcourt (your immediate neighbor) is currently estimated at $1,054,500. Even at our stretch ceiling, you'd still be priced below your direct comp.
  • $949K invites multiple-offer dynamics. Priced ~3–4% under the stretch ceiling creates room for buyer competition without setting up a price reduction. We expect 4–8 offers in the first 10 days at this list.
